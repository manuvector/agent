Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 26, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This application (Manuvector) collects only the information necessary to provide its functionality.

What data we collect

  • Your email address (for authentication and account identification).
  • Access tokens for Google Drive and Notion (only with your explicit consent during connection).
  • Files or pages that you explicitly select from your Google Drive or Notion account. These are processed to extract plain text and create AI embeddings.

How we use your data

  • Selected files are processed temporarily and converted to text for AI-based document search and responses.
  • Only embeddings (mathematical summaries of text) are stored in our database, not the original documents.
  • Tokens are stored securely to let you reuse your connections without logging in each time.

Your control
